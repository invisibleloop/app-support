# Privacy Policy for Squared Chess

_Last updated: April 24, 2026_

Squared Chess is designed with privacy in mind. The app works entirely on your device with no tracking and minimal external services.

## What We Collect

Squared Chess does **not collect or store any personal information** beyond what is described below. All game progress is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following information locally using Apple's standard on-device storage:

- Your game progress and scores
- Your best score
- Your app preferences and settings (haptics, sound effects, onboarding status)

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

## Third-Party Services

Squared Chess uses the following third-party services:

- **Google AdMob** — used to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You will be asked for consent before any personalised ads are shown.

The app may request permission for:

- **Notifications** — optional, used only to send notifications about the game. No notification data is collected or shared.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do **not collect** personal information such as your email or contacts.
- We do **not share** any data with third parties beyond AdMob as described above.
- We do **not use** any analytics or behavioural tracking libraries.
