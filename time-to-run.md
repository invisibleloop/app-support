# Time To Run – Support

**Time To Run** is a simple habit-building running app focused on time, not distance or pace. Set a goal, follow a progressive weekly plan, and build your running habit one week at a time.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

### ❓ How does the app work?
Set an ultimate running goal in minutes, choose how many days a week you want to run, and pick a starting time. The app builds a progressive plan that gradually increases your run time each week until you hit your goal.

### ❓ Does the app use GPS?
No. Time To Run is entirely time-based — no GPS, no distance, no pace. Just you and the clock. This means better battery life and no pressure about how far or fast you run.

### ❓ What happens if I force-quit the app mid-run?
The timer runs in memory, so force-quitting will lose your current run. Use the pause button if you need to stop — the app will keep the timer safe in the background.

### ❓ What if I miss a run?
Your weekly target resets each Monday. Missing a run won't break your plan — just pick up where you left off. Consistency over perfection.

### ❓ Can I run longer than my weekly target?
Absolutely. Runs longer than your target still count towards your weekly goal. Go at your own pace and don't hold back.

### ❓ How do I change my goal?
Tap **Your Goal** on the home screen to update your ultimate goal, starting time, or runs per week at any time.

### ❓ What happens when I complete a plan?
When you finish your final week, you'll get a celebration screen and be guided into setting a new goal. Your next plan will start from where you left off — your first run will be at least as long as your previous ultimate goal.

### ❓ How do badges work?
Badges are earned automatically as you hit milestones — your first run, completing weeks, streaks, and finishing a full plan. Tap the shield icon on the home screen to see your collection.

### ❓ What does the voice coach do?
The voice coach plays audio cues during your run — a 3-2-1 countdown, check-ins at 25%, 50% and 75% of your goal, and a message when you hit your target. You can choose male or female voice and toggle individual cues in Settings.

### ❓ How do I reset all my data?
Go to **Settings → Data → Reset All Data**. This permanently deletes all your runs, goals, and badges. This cannot be undone.
