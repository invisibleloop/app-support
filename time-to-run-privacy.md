# Privacy Policy for Time To Run

_Last updated: April 11, 2026_

Time To Run is designed with privacy in mind. The app works entirely on your device with no accounts, no tracking, and no external services.

## What We Collect

Time To Run does **not collect or store any personal information**. All data you create in the app — including your runs, goals, and badges — is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following information locally using Apple's standard on-device storage:

- Your running goals and weekly progress
- Your run history (duration and date only — no location or route data)
- Your earned badges
- Your app preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

## Third-Party Services

Time To Run uses **no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services**. There are no SDKs collecting data in the background.

The app may request permission for:

- **Notifications** — optional, used only to send run reminders at a time you choose. No notification data is collected or shared.
- **Location** — used only to fetch local weather conditions at the time of your run, displayed in-app only. Your location is never stored or transmitted beyond the weather request.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do **not collect** any personal information such as your name, email, or contacts.
- We do **not share** any data with third parties.
- We do **not use** analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries.
- We do **not store** GPS routes, pace, or distance data of any kind.
